Project Robo

Arduino Arduino IDE C Language Various Sensors

ProjectRobo is made as a welcoming wall-e robot for Quazer v3 2018 a tech-fest conducted by Department of Computer Science, MET'S School of Engineering. We are a team of 8 robot maniacs willing to make this dream into reality. This wall-e robot is a product of our hard work. Everything done for this project is a very new experience for us. We face different kinds of problems at the time of creation and we solved it with group discussions and came-up with new solutions. This project ignite lights in our minds and showed a new possible way of our entertainment. Project is very successful and hence it turns out into a rental item. Robot is rented in a exhibition conducted by a group of students at CentroMall Kodungallur.

Project 3D

Arduino Arduino IDE C Language Various Sensors

Project3D is a competitor winner on Hardware Hackathon program conducted by IEDC RCET, Akkikavu. The theme is to make a prototype that can analyse and identify two different shapes and display its volume. Here Project3D choose to go with Cube and Cylinder as two different shapes. Project3D successfully distinguished between a cube and cylinder and displayed its volume accordingly. Along with that Project3D knows when to calculate and when to stay stand-by.
