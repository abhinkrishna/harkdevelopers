KTU Credit Calculator is a simple tool that helps students under APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) to find their credit score from Semester 1 to Semester 8. This application will make the calculations so simple that they just want to tick the checkboxes corresponding to the subjects they have passed. Application will calculate the credit scores of corresponding subjects and give you credit information on the basis of year out criteria.
